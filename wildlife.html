Debrigarh Wildlife Sanctuary

Debrigarh Wildlife Sanctuary, located along National Highway 59 in Bargarh, holds a unique position in Odisha due to its historical significance. It's known for its association with the renowned freedom fighter Surendra Sai, who took refuge in this dense jungle while planning his rebellion against the British. Besides its historical legacy, Debrigarh is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. Home to the endangered four-horned antelope, the sanctuary boasts a diverse range of species, including tigers, bison, hyenas, leopards, and monitor lizards. The picturesque landscape and fascinating flora and fauna make it a must-visit destination.

Sunabeda Wildlife Sanctuary

Sunabeda Wildlife Sanctuary, situated in the Koraput District, spans over 500 square kilometers and is a proposed Tiger Reserve. This sanctuary features a vast plateau, deep canyons, and 11 stunning waterfalls. It provides a habitat for a wide variety of wildlife species. Its unique landscape and biodiversity make it an ideal destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Hadagarh Wildlife Sanctuary

Hadagarh Wildlife Sanctuary, located just 20 kilometers from Bhadrak in the Kendujhar District, is a place of exceptional natural beauty. Its dense Sal forests are interspersed with vast grasslands, creating a picturesque setting. The sanctuary is known for its breathtaking landscapes, and it's a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. Visitors can witness a range of wildlife amidst the captivating surroundings.
